NBKC Bank

'This is where I currently work. NBKC Bank is a tranditional bank that also offers excellent mortgage services. I have been working here for a year and a half. My first position was called Origination Support Specialist and I worked mainly as a loan officer assistant. Last May, I transfered into the processing department and I have been working as a loan processor ever since. It is a pretty fun place to work. The hours are great and the work itself isn't too stressful. It is a good job to have while I work towards becoming a Software Engineer.'

Michelle O'Connor Real Estate

'This is my mom's real estate company that she has had for 10+ years. I worked here part time while also working the numerous teaching jobs listed below. There were pros and cons to working with my mom. Ultimately it didn't work out and I left and got the job at NBKC Bank.'

Liberty Public Schools

'I was a part time Assistant band director who primarly worked with the high school drumline. This was a really fun position as I was in charge of writing the music, the warmups, and the technique packet. The hours were great - again, it was part time so I worked about 10-15 hours a week here while also working at Michelle O'Connor Real Estate.'

Blue Springs Public Schools

'I worked this position at the same time while working at Liberty Public Schools. It was the same thing - I taught the kids how to kids play drums. However, I didn't write any music or warmups or anything like that. Blue Springs has a reputation for being a very competitive marching band school so they already had those things taken care of. I worked this position in the evenings while working Liberty in the mornings.'

Daras Gas Station

'This was my very last job in college. It was a pretty crappy job with the hours being all over the place for minimum wage. However, it was a college job and they let me do my homework while I was working. I often tell people of the this job while refering to the billion dollar Powerball from a few years back. I had multiple professors come in and drop hundreds on lottery tickets promising me that if they won, they would give me a million bucks. Suffice to say, not a single one of them won.'

Chick Fil A

'This was another college job that I worked. Without a doubt, this was the hardest job I have ever had or will ever have. I worked in the kitchen as a fry cook and it was miserable. I ultimately worked here for about 7 months. I stayed that long because the pay was good and I got free food. If I was able to get free food while I was at work, it was a huge plus to me!'

Chipotle

'This was the job I had the longest in college. Mostly because I was given free chipotle as part of being an employee. That is a huge benefit! This job was not bad and I am still friends with some of the people that I worked with here.'

Menards

'This was a random job to say the least. The only reason I got it was because my roommate at the time was working here and he helped me get the position. I worked in flooring and carpets and, funnily enough, I knew absolutely nothing about either of those things! Either way, this job was alright. It conflicted with my school schedule ultimately and I ended up leaving for the greener pastures of Chipotle.'

Mr. Goodcents

'This was the first company that I worked for. I started when I was in high school and continued on through to my first year of college. Nothing to say about this place. The food was fine. It was my first job - that's about all I have to say!'
